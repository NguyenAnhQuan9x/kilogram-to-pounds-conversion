Kilograms to Pounds Conversion

Pounds (lb) and kilograms (kg) are two important units of mass and weight. The units are used for body weight, produce weight, and many other measurements. How to convert kilograms to pounds is shown in the worked example problem below. This is a typical problem with converting mass units. Let's start with a food example since recipes are one of the most frequent practical uses for this conversion:

Maybe you have a problem: A man weighs 76 kg. What is his weight in pounds?

Start with the conversion factor between pounds and kilograms.

1 kg = 2.2 lbs

Write this in the form of an equation to solve for pounds:

weight in lb = weight in kg / (1 kg / 2.2 lb)

The kilogram cancel out, leaving pounds. In essence this means all you have to do to get a pounds weight in kilogram is multiply by 2.2:

x lbs = 76kg / 1 kg/2.2 lbs
x lbs = 167.2 lbs
